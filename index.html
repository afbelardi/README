Test Your Knowledge of NBA Trivia!

How much do you really know about the NBA?

In this game, you'll have your knowledge tested by answering random trivia questions. Each question is worth 100 points. If you score 200 points or left, you're a rookie. 300 or 400 points and you're an All-Star. Only those who get a perfect score of 500 are Hall of Famers!
